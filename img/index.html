About Us

Founded in 2000, CSI is the oldest technical society in DTU. We aim to provide a platform to learn and share knowledge, inculcate technical skills among students through IDGs, encourage junior-senior interaction and provide exposure to technical events.

CSI has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path since its formative years. Today, the CSI has 71 chapters all over India, 418 student branches, and more than 90000 members including India's most famous IT industry leaders, brilliant scientists and dedicated academicians.
