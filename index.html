About CSI-DTU

Founded in 2000, CSI is the oldest technical society in DTU. We aim to provide a platform to learn and share knowledge, inculcate technical skills among students through IDGs, encourage junior-senior interaction and provide exposure to technical events.

INVICTUS

Invictus is the first edition as the Umbrella Techfest , being organized jointly by all the technical societies of DTU. Carrying on the legacy of the technical fests at DTU , this year's fest will again be a platform for budding technocrats to explore and put their talent to test when they get to compete with other prodigies of their field in the nation. The techfest will cover a wide variety of events mainly in the technical domain with some other miscellaneous events.
